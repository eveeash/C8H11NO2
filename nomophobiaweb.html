DISCONNECT:

A DIGITAL CHALLENGE


We live in a world where technology connects us every second. The fear of being disconnected, known as nomophobia, has grown exponentially. How does it affect our minds and relationships? This challenge is just a first step toward a deeper reflection on the need to disconnect and give ourselves mental space.


We invite you to reflect on your relationship with technology while engaging in this interactive experience.
